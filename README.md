# Dispute-Triage-SLA
The bank has a dispute resolution system in place for the customer transaction disputes pertaining to UPI and Cards
The customers can raise their complaints for their disputed transactions over:
•	Bank’s website
•	Bank’s application
•	By visiting the nearest bank branch
As per the customer satisfaction index received by the bank, the customers are unhappy with the TAT for resolution and with how the dispute status is being communicated to them.
Also, upon research, the bank found that they assign similar priority to the cases based on the days of dispute received irrespective of the amount in question
